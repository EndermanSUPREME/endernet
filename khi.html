    Kent Hack It

    During my time at Kent State I joined the largest KSU Computer Science club HacKSU. HacKSU is a club that teaches anyone, regardless of skill level or major, how to code, along with exposing interested students to other technologies they will not be exposed to in their classes. I became a general leader of this club Fall 2025, as a general leader I would help out by holding lessons and aiding with HacKSU projects such as KHE, a yearly HacKSU sponsored hackathon hosted in the Spring and other potential projects. During the Fall of 2025 we wanted to try hosting a small Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. I helped put together KHI regarding web-development with ReactJS, ExpressJS, Nginx, Docker, as well as helping in creating many of the challenges we plan to serve during October 2025.
