    What's my background?

    I started attempting Digital Capture the Flags in early 2022, I created an account on HackTheBox and started at the starting point for a few and then threw myself at the active machine catagorie. I have no college educational background or any certificates in this field, all of my knowledge is strictly self-taught through playing CTFs and learning from the assistance of others within the community on their public Discord Server.

    My HackTheBox Profile

    I occasionally upload my solution to boxes that HackTheBox labels as retired, due to their TOS asking that write-ups/solutions to active machines are not to be released before the retire date. I upload boxes I have completed occasionally in large batches.

    I try to actively participate in the HackTheBox Seasonal CTF competition to both learn more and have fun finishing new boxes.
