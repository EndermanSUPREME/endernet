    C++ Keylogger

    Within my close family I am the most tech savy, I have some cousins on my dad's side that are also programmers, but concerning close I'm the only one that understands programming.

    I was told to lockdown my siblings laptops, standard children internet protections, add filters censor/monitor internet connection and so forth. I wanted to have fun with this so I took things a bit more advanced than needed, but I ended up settling on a KeyLogger implementation my Keylogger tracks the keystrokes and records data into hidden files, when the user hits the enter-key I parse all of the words typed and check if any of them are within a black-list I put together, and if a word gets flagged it will trigger a silent alarm and take both a screenshot of the laptop screen and send an email to a parent showing both what word triggered the email and the laptop screenshot using the following powershell-scripts.
