    Game Development

    What have I done?

    I started learning Unity in 2019 during my Junior year of high school, I started by giving myself a theme and only a weeks time to pull it off, I learned through many tutorials on youtube and experimenting on my own.

    I eventually decided to pursue the video game design and development major at Stark State College in Ohio. On my freetime outside of school and work I would try to actively participate in GameJams on Itch.io and I made a fair number of games, my releases of CHAT were my biggest personal projects I completed.

    I still try to create more games and publish them on Itch.io, but being a solo-developer most of the time makes projects take a long time to complete, especially when I have to model, rig, animate, and texture any armatures myself on Blender.
