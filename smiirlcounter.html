    Smiirl Counter

    A group of friends at KSU wanted to convert this popular subscriber/follower counter into a clock they can use. They took the casing apart and I quickly noticed looking at the board that there was a UART connector. I'd never performed a hardware hack before tackling this device, I got a special cable to interact with UART through a bare-metal Kali Linux OS and was able to get into the device and reconfigure the device to their needs. I also dug around and managed to find a OpenWRT SDK tool-chain where I can compile my own C code and run it inside the device's MIPS Processor. Main goal is using custom C to interact with the I2C motors the device uses.
